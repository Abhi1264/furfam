# Our Commitment to Ethical Breeding: The FurFam Promise

At FurFam, we believe that every dog deserves to be born into a loving, healthy, and ethical environment. Our commitment to ethical breeding isn't just a promise—it's the foundation of everything we do. This is our story, our standards, and our commitment to you and the dogs we help bring into your family.

## What Ethical Breeding Means to Us

Ethical breeding goes far beyond simply producing puppies. It encompasses:

### Health and Genetic Testing

**Our Standards:**
- All breeding dogs undergo comprehensive health screenings
- Genetic testing for breed-specific conditions
- Regular veterinary care and checkups
- Health guarantees for all puppies
- Transparent health records

**Why It Matters:**
- Prevents hereditary diseases
- Ensures healthy puppies
- Reduces suffering
- Protects your investment
- Promotes breed health

### Proper Care and Living Conditions

**Our Requirements:**
- Clean, spacious living environments
- Proper nutrition and feeding schedules
- Regular exercise and socialization
- Mental stimulation and enrichment
- Comfortable, safe facilities

**Our Commitment:**
- No puppy mills or commercial breeding facilities
- Regular facility inspections
- High standards for all partners
- Continuous improvement

### Responsible Breeding Practices

**What We Do:**
- Appropriate breeding ages and frequencies
- Proper rest periods between litters
- Limited breeding to prevent overbreeding
- Retirement plans for breeding dogs
- Lifetime care commitment

**What We Don't Do:**
- Never support puppy mills
- Never work with irresponsible breeders
- Never compromise on health standards
- Never prioritize profit over welfare

## How You Can Support Ethical Breeding

### As a Potential Owner

- Research breeds thoroughly
- Ask questions about health and breeding
- Visit facilities when possible
- Choose ethical sources
- Commit to responsible ownership

### Red Flags to Avoid

- Unwillingness to show facilities
- No health testing or records
- Multiple litters available
- Pressure to buy quickly
- Unusually low prices
- No health guarantees

### Questions to Ask

- Can I see health records?
- Where are the parents?
- What health testing is done?
- Can I visit the facility?
- What support do you provide?
- What's included in adoption?
